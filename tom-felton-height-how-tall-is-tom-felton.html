    Tom Felton stands tall at a height of 5 feet 9 inches (1.75 m). He is an English actor best known for playing Draco Malfoy in the film adaptations of the Harry Potter fantasy novels by J. K. Rowling.

    Tom Felton portrayed Doctor Alchemy on The CW’s The Flash, based on the comic books of the same name. Tom Felton co-starred in drama film Feed (2017), action-thriller Stratton (2017), and biographical film Megan Leavey (2017).

    Tom Felton appeared in Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (2004) and Harry Potter and Goblet of Fire. Felton made a guest appearance on Home Farm Twins in 2005, where he took on the role of Adam Baker in the short-lived series.

    Tom Felton appeared in the sci-fi film Rise of the Planet of the Apes (2011) and the indie films From the Rough (2011) and The Apparition (2012).

    Tom Felton was a series regular on the 2018 sci-fi series Origin and appeared as Laertes in Claire McCarthy’s Ophelia (2018). Felton portrayed the villain in family horror A Babysitter’s Guide to Monster Hunting (2020).
