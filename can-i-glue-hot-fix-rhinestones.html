    The simple answer is yes. You may add glue to a Hot Fix Rhinestone. But why would you want to. First of all, the link between the glue you are adhering and the adhesive already on the back of the stone is not as strong as if you applied glue straight to foiling or if you merely put glue to the back of a no Hot Fix stone.
