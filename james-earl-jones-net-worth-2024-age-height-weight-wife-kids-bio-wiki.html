    Early Life

    James Earl Jones was born on January 17, 1931, in Arkabutla, Mississippi, United States. His father, Robert Earl Jones, was an actor and fighter, but he died in 2006, and his mother, Ruth Connolly, worked as a teacher. Robert left the family after James’s birth. James has a stepbrother from his father’s other marriage, and his name is Matthew Earl Jones.

    James Earl Jones was mostly grown and raised by his grandparents from his mother’s side. He studied at Brethren High School, which was called Dickson Rural Agricultural School. James started as a pre-medical student at the University of Michigan and then changed his major to drama at the School of Music, Theatre & Dance, the University of Michigan, finishing and graduating in 1955. Later, James had some time studying at the American Theatre Wing in New York.
