    Kody Cephus is the celebrity child popular as the son of a famous entertainment personality Offset and his ex-love partner Oriel Jamie.

    The celebrity son Kody was born on March 2, 2015, in Los Angeles, California, USA, under the zodiac sign of Pisces. As of now, Kody is living lavish and limelight life with her mother and also frequently visits his father. 

    Parents: Offset & Oriel

    Kody was born silver spoon in his mouth. Since birth, he is living a spotlight life. Kody's father Kiari Kendrell Cephus, widely popular by his nickname Offset is an American rapper and songwriter.

    The 28-years-old Offset is one of the three members of the hip hop music group Migos. The group Migos has released songs such as Versace, Fight Night, Bad and Boujee, Pure  Water, etc. The Grammy Awards nominated rapper Offset has also starred in the series like Atlanta, and NCIS: Los Angeles.

    On the other hand, Kody's mum Oriel is a businesswoman known as the founder of the hair salon Jamie's Studio (JS) The JS offers hair extension, eyebrow tinting, and was services. Moreover, she is also an Instagram star who has amassed more than 80,000 followers on her Instagram account.

    How and when Oriel & Offset met and began their romantic affair it is still unclear. But the report claims that the duo dated from 2013 to 2016. The detailed information regarding their relationship still lacks in the media. 

    Offset's Marital Bond With Cardi B

    The five-years-old celebrity son Kody's father Offset was in a marital relationship with Belcalis Marlenis Almanzar, known by her stage name Cardi B. Like Offset, Cardi B is the rapper, singer, songwriter.

    Cardi B and Offset officially began dating back in 2017 after he proposed her at a live performance in Philadelphia. Then on April 7, 2018, she revealed that she is expecting her first child with Offset.

    In June 2018, it was disclosed that the rappers Offset & Cardi B were secretly married in September 2017. Later, she also confirmed their marriage via Instagram post. She and Offset shared their first kid, a daughter, Kulture Kiari Cephus, on July 10, 2018.

    Everything was going fine; however, in December 2018, Offset and Cardi B temporarily separated. The duo later reconciles and in January 2020, they also attended the 62nd Annual Grammy Award function together. But again, as of September 2020, it is reported that Cardi B has filed for divorce.

    Half-Siblings

    Besides Kulture, Kody has two other half-siblings Jordan Cephus and Kalea Marie Cephus. Jordan was born on December 21, 2010, to Offset and his former love partner Justine Watson.

    Similarly, Kalea who was born on March 13, 2015, is the daughter of rapper Offset and the musical artist Shy L'amour. Kody shares a good bond with his half-sisters and half-brother.

    Enjoys Parent's Net Worth

    Kody is in his childhood. so more than involving in any professional works, he is busy with his studies. At the moment, his wealthy father has provided him enough facilities to live life lavishly.

    Kody's father Offset owns a fortune of $26 million which he accumulated from his successful career as a rapper. As per Celebrity Net Worth, the trio group Migos earned around $36 million between September 2018 to September 2019.
