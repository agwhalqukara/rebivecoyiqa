    Duchess Kate may be in for a lifetime of royal treatment, but that doesn't mean she's opposed to making repeats in her wardrobe.

    The 29-year-old stepped out in London on June 24 wearing the same black faux wrap dress by Issa that she wore on an April 20th shopping trip (pictured left), nine days before her wedding day.

    This isn't the first time the Duchess has re-worn one of her stylish getups. She recycled her blue Jacquard coat dress at Prince Philip's 90th birthday party on June 12, which she had originally worn to a wedding in 2009.

    Since Kate's Issa LBD is no longer available, we found three different styles so you can snag a similar version for a fraction of the original's price (480 GBP; $768).
