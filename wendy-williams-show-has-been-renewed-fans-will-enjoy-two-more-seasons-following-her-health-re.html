    As you all know, Wendy Williams struggled with some health problems lately, but despite everything, it seems that the show’s fans can relax because the show will go on.

    For the past few weeks, Wendy has been acting really strange during her show. The problems included slurred speech and tumbling onstage.

    It was also reported that Wendy called out of work for unknown health reasons.

    Recently, viewers started to notice what has been going on and Wendy decided to reveal the cause behind all this.

    ‘As I reported earlier in the week, I have a hairline fracture on my upper arm, which hurts like hell,’ she said, among others.

    Now, Wendy’s fans receive great news, just ahead of the New Year’s Eve.

    A source close to the host revealed more details regarding Wendy and her show. They spilled the tea to the popular online magazine Hollywood Life.

    ‘Wendy is doing well and is excited about the future of her show,’ the source said.

    ‘Despite sitting down occasionally, an issue she has already addressed, Wendy’s health is fine, and she’s not going anywhere. (…) Wendy is doing great,’ the same insider reported.

    They also explained that her marriage is st5ronger than ever. More than that, it seems that her show has been renewed until 2021.

    So, as you can see, the controversial host will enter fine in 2019.

    The news is great especially after the hectic year that Wendy had. She has also been slammed a lot by various celebrities, the latest one being 50 Cent as we already reported.
